Careers at Innoscape

Grow with us. Build more than landscapes. Build your future.

At Innoscape, we don’t just shape beautiful outdoor spaces—we cultivate careers rooted in creativity, craftsmanship, and care. Whether you're just getting started or bringing years of experience, we offer a supportive environment where your skills can thrive and your passion can flourish.

Our Hiring Process

We believe the best teams are built with purpose. Here’s what to expect when applying to Innoscape:

  1. Application: Submit your resume and a cover letter outlining your experience and passion for landscaping.

  2. Interview: If selected, you’ll be invited for an interview to discuss your skills and fit for our team.

  3. Assessment: Depending on the position, you may be asked to complete a practical assessment to showcase your abilities.

  4. Offer: Successful candidates will receive a formal job offer and details about joining our team.

What to expect from us

  • Support: We provide ongoing training and development opportunities to help you grow in your career.

  • Collaboration: Work alongside a team of passionate professionals who value your input and ideas.

  • Innovation: Be part of a company that encourages creativity and embraces new technologies in landscaping.
